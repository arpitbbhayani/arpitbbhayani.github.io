Some protocols and methods that help us achieve this
  • Native Sockets

REST

Sending request and getting response over HTTP. It can optionally use HTTP verbs to actuate the implicit meaning.

The primary reason of REST becoming huge was its

  • Simplicity
  • Ease of extension
  • Use of HTTP as a channel

Problems with REST

  • Endpoint Explosion
  • Rely and Maintain A Good Documentation
  • Multiple Requests
  • Over Fetching
  • Under Fetching
  • Special Endpoints For Specific Purpose
